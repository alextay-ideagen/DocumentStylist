Human Resources Marketing

The CompliSpace Human Resources Module is available for your school to subscribe to.

About this Module:

The Human Resources Module has been designed to assist schools to better manage the entire employment lifecycle from Recruitment, through to Training and Development and Growth through to Exit. All our policies are kept up-to-date with legal and regulatory changes. These include:

  • Enterprise Agreement (EA) Mapping - translation of key EA terms into plain English policies. If your school has an EA and we haven’t mapped it, this will be included in our set-up process.

  • General Conditions of Employment - a comprehensive library of general employment policies including BYO Device, Dress Code, Email and Internet Usage, Gifts and Social Media Usage. If we don’t have a policy you are looking for, our expert team will draft it for you.

  • Employee Leave Entitlements - mapped to the Fair Work Act and awards, and to specific jurisdictional requirements where they apply. If you have an EA, we map that into plain English policies.

  • Bullying, Discrimination and Harassment - including guidance for staff as to what to do if they believe that they have been subject to inappropriate behaviour.

  • Internal Grievance Resolution - designed to establish a consistent and transparent approach to dealing with conflicts in the workplace, internal complaints about the work environment.

There’s also a secure HR Administration Section designed to assist HR administrators manage internal processes such as:

  • Recruitment procedures with associated documentation that is designed to meet the requirements of the National Principles for Child Safe Organisations the National Catholic Safeguarding Standards, anti-discrimination, and privacy requirements

  • Discipline & Termination guidance, which include performance management, redundancy, summary dismissal and notice periods

  • Performance Monitoring, Professional Development and Performance Reviews applying best practice child safe principles

  • Induction

  • Record-keeping

As part of the HR Module, through the CompliLearn Staff Learning System, we provide an online HR Training Program to assist your school to effectively communicate the key elements of the Module to all staff. The HR learning suite covers:

  • Introduction to HR (General Policies and Procedures)

  • Bullying, Discrimination and Harassment

  • Internal Grievance Procedures

  • Social Media Usage

  • Managing Personal and Confidential Information.

The HR Module includes related risk management and compliance content that is delivered through our Assurance product. The HR Module Assurance content includes:

  • An HR Risk and Control Task Framework

  • Staff Credentialing

  • A suite of HR-related forms and checklists including a:

  • Leave Form

  • Expense Claim Form

  • Travel Request Form

  • Education Assistance Application Form

  • Flexible Working Arrangements Form

  • Membership Subscription Form.
